The Unseen Challenge

India faces over 3 crore stray animals causing road accidents, public health risks, and inefficiency in animal management.

🚗 Road Accidents
Frequent and fatal incidents involving animals and humans.
🧫 Public Health Risks
Spread of diseases due to unhygienic conditions.
📍 Lack of Tracking
No unified system for rescue or vaccination monitoring.

Our Innovative Solution

StraySense introduces the StrayTrack Band — a GPS-based, neoprene wearable for safe and effective stray animal tracking.

  • Real-time tracking and monitoring via cloud and mobile app
  • Health sensors integrated for animal wellbeing
  • Wireless data updates with low power consumption
